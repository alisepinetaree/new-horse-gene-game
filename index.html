How It Works

  • Breeding randomly selects one allele from each parent for every gene.
  • The starter calculates a simple phenotype from extension, agouti, cream, dun, and gray.
  • Saved foals stay in your browser using localStorage.
  • You can expand this with more genes, art, markets, exploration, and registries.
